Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Inventory handles your data.

App: Inventory
Last updated: July 6, 2026

Summary: Inventory is designed to store inventory records locally on your Mac. The app does not sell your data, does not use advertising SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites.

1. Information We Collect

Inventory does not intentionally collect personal information from users. The information you enter into the app, such as inventory items, serial numbers, categories, comments, QR codes, labels, imported CSV data, and related records, is stored locally on your device unless you choose to export, copy, print, or share it.

2. Local Data Storage

Your inventory database is stored locally on your Mac. Inventory does not upload your inventory database to our servers. If you choose to place your database, exported files, backups, or CSV files in cloud storage, network storage, or another external location, that storage provider may process your data according to its own privacy policy.

3. Import, Export, QR Codes, and Labels

The app may allow you to import and export CSV files, generate QR codes, and print labels. These actions are performed to provide the app’s core functionality. You control the files you import, export, save, print, or share.

4. Subscriptions and Purchases

Inventory may offer subscriptions or in-app purchases. Payments and subscription management are handled by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

5. Network Access

Inventory may use network access for features such as subscription validation or user-selected storage/synchronization scenarios. The app does not use network access to sell personal information or deliver third-party advertising.

6. Analytics and Advertising

Inventory does not include third-party advertising SDKs. Inventory does not sell your data. If analytics are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe what is collected and why.

7. Support Requests

If you contact us for support, we may receive information you choose to provide, such as your email address, app version, device details, screenshots, or a description of the issue. This information is used only to respond to your request and improve the app.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may be processed by Apple when you use App Store subscriptions or in-app purchases. Data may also be processed by third-party services you choose to use, such as cloud storage, network storage, email, or file-sharing services.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Inventory data remains on your device until you delete it, export it, move it, or uninstall the app. You are responsible for managing your own exported files, backups, and imported documents.

10. Children’s Privacy

Inventory is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Security

We use Apple platform features and standard macOS security practices where applicable. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. You should protect your Mac, backups, exported files, and network storage with appropriate passwords and access controls.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
